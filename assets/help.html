Driver Theory Test Ireland: Car and Motorcycle - DTT Questions

Contains the complete DTT question bank for car and motorcycle drivers in Ireland.

The application is divided into the following sections:

Mock Theory Test
Perform a simulation under the same conditions as the official RSA test. You have 45 minutes to answer 40 questions of the test. To pass the exam you have to answer 35 of 40 questions correctly. When you finish the test you will see your score and review all the questions. View full explanations after each question to help you remember the correct answer for next time.

Practice by Topics
Test your knowledge by practicing by categories. The bank of questions from the RSA is divided into 14 categories.

You can select one or more categories to practice. In this section there is no time limit and you can see the explanation before selecting the correct answer.

Review All Questions
The entire question bank of questions presented to you by category.

Progress Monitor
The application saves the result of each question answered and a history of failures and successes to offer the most advanced statistics system.
